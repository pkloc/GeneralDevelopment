About Orchesis Modern Dance

Orchesis is a modern and jazz dance program offered through the University of Alberta - Campus Recreation to dancers of all abilities and experience. Registration is open to the students, public and alumni.

Those who register in any of Orchesis Modern Dance Classes participate in dance training through weekly modern and jazz dance classes. Modern dance classes cover various technical styles of modern dance with floorwork, barre exercises and traveling movement phrases, accompanied by live musicians. Jazz classes emphasize the practice and perfecting of technique, within the jazz idiom. All classes consist of preparatory exercises, locomotor work across the floor, stretching and the learning of dance phrase combinations.

Dance Motif is our annual concert showcasing Orchesis choreography and talent. There is an optional performance opportunity for those who register for the ANNUAL membership. Registrants who sign up for one term only may not participate in the annual concert.

Orchesis Dance Society's Role

Orchesis Dance Society (ODS) is a not-for-profit society raising awareness about Orchesis Modern Dance and providing funding for Orchesis shows, events and activities in Edmonton, such as Dance Motif.

ODS members will receive exclusive member rates to events and can earn points they can use towards sportswear, merchandise and ODS membership. Anyone can help ODS by donating their ideas, time, donating unwanted items to the Orchesis Garage Sale.
