Becoming A Member

Annual Fee: $5.00

Becoming a member means that you are supporting the Orchesis Dance Group. Your membership fee will be used for things like grants for dancers and choreographers, extra costs of Motif and the Orchesis Summer Dance Intensives.

To become a member, fill out a membership form and submit a hardcopy with your $5.00 payment to:

Orchesis Dance Society, c/o Tamara Bliss

Edmonton, AB
