Orchesis Dance Group Presents:

Dance Motif '2015'

50th anniversary performance and Gala

The Orchesis Dance Group's 50th anniversary will be celebrated with a GALA EVENT. Dance Motif 2015 showcases our dancers, with special guests and faculty choreographers in a concert to remember. Saturday night, please join us as we continue the celebration following the 3 PM matinee with the Gala Event: cocktails, dinner, special program, silent auction, dancing! The live band 'MIXTAPE' will provide music for the evening.

Guest choreographers, Peggy Baker and Brian Webb, whose participation is made possible by support from The Alberta Foundation for the Arts, and the Orchesis Dance Society, the non-profit society supporting Orchesis Dance Group

Featuring modern and jazz choreography by local dance artists.

Performance details:


At the Myer Horowitz Theatre, Students' Union Building, University of Alberta

* Friday, January 30, 2015 at 8:00 p.m.

* Saturday, January 31, 2015 matinee at 3:00 p.m.
